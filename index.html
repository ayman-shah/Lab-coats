Lab Coats

Home

A Lab Coat, also known as a white coat, is an item of clothing usually worn in labarotaries and by professionals in the medical field. It is long white comfortable overcoat, knee-length and quite comfortable. Worn by laboratory workers and doctors to protect their street clothing from damage, as a uniform, and is also very popular.
Lab Coats first appeared in the early 1800s, as a uniform for medical and scientific professionals.
Many TV shows and movie feature lab coats and they are usually an important part of a characters design, such as Frankenstein's Monster, Spiderman, Rick and Morty and many more. Lab coats also appear in many games, sometimes as part of a character's design, other times as an item or piece of equipment like in Fallout 4 and SCP.
