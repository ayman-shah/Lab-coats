Lab Coats

Information

What is a Lab Coat?

A Lab Coat is a an item of clothing commnoly worn in laboratories by scientists and by medical professionals on the job. It is an item to keep wearer's safe as well as a uniform fro medical and scientific professionals

Uses of A Lab Coat?

Lab Coat's can be and are used for many things, of these the most common are as safety equipment in labs and hospitals, as uniforms by scientific and medical professionals, as gear to protect clothign in lab environments, because it's comfortable and to look clean and sterile or cool.

Why are Lab Coats Important?

Lab Coats are very important, especially when in use by medical and scientific professionals as they are their symbol. Lab Coats comfort many patients, since they have this image of cleanliness and are very neat looking. They protect the skin and clothing in labs from chemicals or other dangers and also help to identify dangers, like contaminants or dirt if any get on it.

Features of a Lab Coat?

The coat protects their street clothes and also serves as a simple uniform. The garment is made from white or light-colored cotton, linen, or cotton polyester blend, making it comfortable, allowing it to be washed at high temperature and making it easy to see if it is clean.Lab Coats usually are long sleeved and some are short sleeved, some have buttons or elastics at the end of the sleeves to secure them around the wrist so the do not hang into containers of chemicals. They are also made of absorbant material to protect from chemicals and have pockets to carry things.
